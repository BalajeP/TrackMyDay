# How to Install Track My Day as an App

Track My Day can be installed on your phone or laptop just like Amazon, Flipkart, or any other app!

## 🎯 Benefits of Installing

- **Quick Access**: Launch directly from your home screen with one tap/click
- **Full Screen**: Works like a native app without browser chrome
- **Offline Support**: Continue using even with poor connectivity
- **No App Store**: Install directly from the web - no download needed

---

## 📱 Install on Mobile (Android/iOS)

### Android (Chrome/Edge/Samsung Internet)

1. Open the app in your browser
2. Look for the **"Install Now"** button on the login page, OR
3. Tap the **menu (⋮)** in your browser
4. Select **"Add to Home Screen"** or **"Install app"**
5. Confirm the installation
6. The app icon will appear on your home screen

### iPhone/iPad (Safari)

1. Open the app in Safari
2. Tap the **Share button** (box with arrow pointing up)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Name it "Track My Day" (or your preference)
5. Tap **"Add"**
6. The app icon will appear on your home screen

---

## 💻 Install on Desktop (Windows/Mac/Linux)

### Chrome, Edge, or Brave

1. Open the app in your browser
2. Look for the **install icon (⊕)** in the address bar, OR
3. Click on the **Download icon** in the app header (when logged in), OR
4. Click the **menu (⋮)** → **"Install Track My Day"**
5. Confirm the installation
6. The app will open in its own window and appear in your app launcher

### Safari (Mac)

1. Open the app in Safari
2. Click **File** → **"Add to Dock"**
3. The app will be pinned to your Dock for quick access

---

## 🔍 How to Identify if Installation is Available

When you open the app:

- **On the login page**: You'll see a banner with "Install Track My Day" and an "Install Now" button
- **When logged in**: Look for a **Download icon** in the top-right corner of the app
- **In your browser**: Look for an install prompt or icon in the address bar

---

## ✅ Features After Installation

Once installed:

- Opens in a standalone window (no browser tabs/address bar)
- Appears in your app drawer/launcher
- Can be pinned to taskbar/dock
- Works offline with cached data
- Receives updates automatically

---

## 🗑️ How to Uninstall

### Mobile
- **Android**: Long-press the app icon → **"Uninstall"**
- **iOS**: Long-press the app icon → **"Remove App"**

### Desktop
- **Windows/Linux**: Right-click app in Start/Launcher → **"Uninstall"**
- **Mac**: Right-click app in Dock → **"Options"** → **"Remove from Dock"**
- Or uninstall via Chrome: **Settings** → **"Apps"** → Find "Track My Day" → **"Uninstall"**

---

## 💡 Tips

- You can install on multiple devices and stay synced via your account
- The app updates automatically when you open it
- All your data is stored securely in Supabase cloud storage
- No internet? The app still works with your previously loaded data
